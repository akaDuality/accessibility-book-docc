# Practice

Exercises for getting comfortable with VoiceOver — turn it on and try using the phone.

## Tasks

Do the following exercises to reinforce what you've learned:

1. **Turn on VoiceOver.** You can use a Siri command to turn it off; check in advance that she's working.

2. **Set up a shortcut** for quick access via the side button or by tapping the back of the phone.

3. **Use the standard iOS apps**, see how they read buttons, their names, and types.

4. **Learn to minimize apps** and open the notification panel.

5. **Try turning off the screen** and orient yourself by sound only.

6. **Try using your own app**, note the unclear places, where there are no labels, where there are many controls, and where the focus is broken.

7. **Can you complete the main scenario** in your app?

## What's next

At this stage, it's worth writing down all the unclear places in your app. Later you'll understand how to fix them.

> Tip: If you don't have your own app, take any popular app from the App Store. An example of good adaptation can be seen in the Dodo Pizza app.
