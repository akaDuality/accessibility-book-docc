## Overview

Accessibility of an application is the adaptation of its interface for different groups of people. If a person sees poorly, can't hear, can't move, and yet still uses the application fully — then it is accessible.

This book is about the most severe impairments, where at first it isn't even clear how a person could use a touchscreen phone at all. You'll learn how VoiceOver works, how to adapt the interface, and how to verify the result.

If you don't have time to read the whole book and just want one article, then read [How to make an iOS app more accessible? A big and detailed guide](https://vc.ru/design/1919956-dostupnost-ios-prilozheniy) (in Russian).
